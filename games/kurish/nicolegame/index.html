Game using the TwoPointFive Plugin for Impact

Rotate to Landscape to play!

Nostalgia is a first-person shooter, made with the engine ImpactJS using the TwoPointFive plugin. The game is more about the aesthetic experience, so right now there is no final objective. My theme is 90's nostalgia, and I incorporated things that symbolized adolescence during that period: Barney, the Teletubbies, Flintstones vitamins, and Tamagotchis. My inspiration for Nostalgia came from watching my dad play a first-person shooter when I was young. For me, the girl who follows the shooter represents me watching my dad play. From an outside perspective, the girl is a reflection of the shooter as a child. Shoot, watch, whichever you want.
